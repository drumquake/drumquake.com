Sander Owen is a Norwegian drummer living in the USA.

He has played with Kevin Moore (Dream Theater), Chris Poland (Megadeth), Matt Malley (Counting Crows), Ric Fierabracci (Chick Corea), Brett Garsed (Nelson), Ben Mallare (Kelly Clarkson), Vartan Mkhitaryan (Apple Pie), Marie Bergman (the Swedish artist), The Gravity Guild (top ten Hollywood band on ReverbNation), and many more.

He has also produced music for The Red Cross, Keith Floyd (the famous TV chef), TV Norge, and Norsk Film. In Los Angeles, he has performed at The Whisky a Go Go, The Baked Potato, Musician's Institute, and more.

He started playing the drums at age five because no one else wanted to be the drummer in his older brothers Kiss cover band. He quickly fell in love with the instrument, and started learning from the best--Omar Hakim, Manu Katche, Jeff Porcaro, Dennis Chambers, Peter Erskine, Stewart Copeland, Dave Weckl, Steve Gadd, Bernard Peardy, Jimmy Cobb, Billy Higgins.

At fifteen, he started doing professional work--cruise ships, wedding bands, cover bands. He also had many students at that time. He went on to study music at SVG School under the Norwegian jazz drummer Audun Kleive (Terje Rypdal).

"Sander is a great drummer, gifted songwriter,
and a true professional".

Matt Malley (Counting Crows)
